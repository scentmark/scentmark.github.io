Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2025

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy outlines how your information is handled.

1. Information Collected

We collect structural order details when you submit forms on our website. This includes your: Full Name, Phone Number, Email Address (optional), Delivery Address, and payment verification details (like Transaction IDs for mobile banking).

2. Use of Information

All collected data is utilized purely to fulfill and record customer orders. We use these entries to structure and populate sheets via Google Apps Script integration and to compose WhatsApp templates which assist transactional delivery communication.

3. Data Storage & Safety

Our website processes and transmits your checkout details via secure endpoints directly to Google Sheets database APIs. Your cart contents are securely cached locally within your browser using localStorage mechanisms.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose personal details to external advertisers. Transaction payloads are only routed directly to WhatsApp for automated messaging context and our secure Google Drive Sheets.

5. User Rights

Should you wish to review, alter, or request structural deletion of any personal delivery details stored, reach out directly with proof of ID to: scentmark@gmail.com.
